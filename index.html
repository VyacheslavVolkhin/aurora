Withstands thermal shocks and thermal cycles

Our products are manufactured using unique technology of oxidative design in ceramics. Plant consists of a modified incinerator and a unit for high-temperature cleaning of flue gases. Combustion of soot and organic compounds occurs in the channels of the ceramic honeycomb blocks; a ceramic heat exchanger is installed to cool the flow and recover the heat.
